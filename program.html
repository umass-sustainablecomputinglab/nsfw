Program

The workshop program will extend over 1.5 days and will be organized around 4 topics drawn from the position papers. There will be a plenary panel session on each topic that will seed per-topic breakout sessions, where attendees will further discuss the topic to construct recommendations for NSF regarding the vision, challenges, and opportunities for research on the topic over the next 5-10 years. A program schedule is below.

Plenary sessions will be held in the Mackenzie Room MacKenzie room in the Jen-Hsun Huang Engineering Center (475 Via Ortega, Room 300, Stanford University, Stanford, CA 94305). Breakout sessions will be held in separate rooms per topic. Working dinner will be at P.F.Chang's (900 Stanford Shopping Center, Bldg. W, Palo Alto, CA 94304).
