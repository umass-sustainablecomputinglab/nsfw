Venue

The workshop will be held on campus at Stanford University.

Plenary sessions will be in the Mackenzie Room in the Jen-Hsun Huang Engineering Center located at 475 Via Ortega, Room 300, Stanford, CA 94305. Directions here.

Breakout sessions will be in the Gates Building located at 353 Serra Mall, Stanford, CA 94305 with per-topic rooms listed in the program. Directions from plenary sessions to the breakout session rooms are here.

Working dinner will be at P.F.Chang's (900 Stanford Shopping Center, Bldg. W, Palo Alto, CA 94304).

Parking Directions

You should park in Stanford Parking Structure #2 (also called the Via Ortega Garage) at the corner of Panama St. and Via Ortega. The address for your GPS is: 285 Panama St, Stanford, CA 94305.

We have 10 parking passes for reserved spots in this lot. Please email the chairs if you would like one of these parking passes. If you do not have a parking pass (or we run out), you may park in the same garage on a pay-by-space basis. You may pay for parking at any machine in the lot or structure ($2.00 - $2.50 per hour), which the workshop can reimburse. Parking permit machines require payment by Visa, MasterCard, or Discover card only; no cash accepted. In most cases a 30-minute minimum payment is required. A map showing the parking garage, plenary building, and breakout session building is linked here.

Note that it is possible that the parking garage may fill up, although this is unlikely if you arrive before 8am for the Continental Breakfast. If this occurs, there are a number of other parking garages and places available on campus. More information on Stanford visitor parking is here.
